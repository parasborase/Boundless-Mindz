Why Boundless Minds works

Our major aim is to address the exclusion problem resulting from no accessible educational platforms, which excludes an important portion of the student population. We are aware that a large number of standardized educational websites do not cater to the varied demands of disabled students, thus limiting their access to education. In that light, our project holds this matter to be of extremely important consequence and endeavours to close this gap through the integration of features that may be universally applied, to mention but a few friendly navigation, adjustable fonts, and compatibility with screen readers.
